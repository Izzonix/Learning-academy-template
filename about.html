About Sunrise Academy

Discover our commitment to excellence and holistic education

Our Mission

To empower students with a strong academic foundation and moral values, nurturing them to become responsible, innovative, and successful global citizens.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of excellence in education, known for innovation, inclusiveness, and transformative learning experiences that impact society.

Our History

Founded in 2008, Sunrise Academy began as a small community school with a vision to make quality education accessible. Over the years, we have grown into a leading institution recognized for academic excellence, extracurricular achievements, and a nurturing environment that brings out the best in every learner.
